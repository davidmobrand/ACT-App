About the ACT Therapy Assistant

The ACT Therapy Assistant is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help you explore and practice Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) principles. While it's not a replacement for professional therapy, it serves as a supportive tool for learning and practicing ACT concepts.

Available in multiple Nordic languages (English, Danish, Swedish, Norwegian, Icelandic, and Finnish), the assistant aims to make ACT principles more accessible to a wider audience.

What is ACT?

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) is a form of psychotherapy that emphasizes psychological flexibility. It helps individuals accept what is outside their control and commit to actions that enrich their lives.

The Six Core Processes of ACT:

1. Acceptance

Embracing your thoughts and feelings without trying to change them

2. Cognitive Defusion

Learning to step back and observe thoughts rather than getting caught up in them

3. Being Present

Engaging with the here-and-now experience with openness and curiosity

4. Self as Context

Accessing a transcendent sense of self beyond thoughts and feelings

5. Values

Identifying what truly matters to you in life

6. Committed Action

Taking effective action guided by your values
